# Case Study: Nhà Hàng Phở Modern "Phở Ơi" - Hà Nội

> **Áp dụng**: Blue Ocean Strategy, Pricing Strategy, Digital Marketing, CLV Analysis

---

## I. TỔNG QUAN

### Background
- **Founder**: Chị Lan (35 tuổi, chef training tại Le Cordon Bleu)
- **Concept**: Modernize phở - traditional taste, contemporary presentation & service
- **Location**: Đống Đa, Hà Nội (near universities + office buildings)
- **Open**: Tháng 11/2025 (đang hoạt động 6 tháng)
- **Initial Investment**: 800M VNĐ

### The Challenge
Sau 6 tháng, doanh thu chỉ đạt 60% target. Traffic tốt giờ cao điểm (sáng, trưa) nhưng chiều tối vắng khách. Cần chiến lược tăng trưởng.

## II. BLUE OCEAN STRATEGY - TÁI TẠO THỊ TRƯỜNG PHỞ

### 1. Strategy Canvas - Current State

```
Value High│
      │   Traditional        Modern
      │   Phở              Restaurant
      │      ╱              ╱
      │     ╱   PHỞ ƠI    ╱
      │    ╱      │      ╱
      │   ╱       │     ╱
Low   │  ╱        │    ╱
      └──┼────────┼───┼──────────────→
         Price  Quality  Ambiance  Service  Menu   Speed
                                           Variety
```

**Analysis**:
- Competing trong "red ocean" với traditional phở (giá) và modern restaurants (quality + ambience)
- Chưa tạo được "blue ocean" - unique value space
- Need to **ERRC** (Eliminate-Reduce-Raise-Create)

### 2. ERRC Grid - Strategy Redesign

| ELIMINATE | REDUCE |
|-----------|--------|
| ❌ Paper menu (waste, outdated) | 🔽 Kitchen staff (3 → 2, automate some prep) |
| ❌ Physical cashier (use QR ordering) | 🔽 Rent cost (renegotiate or relocate back-kitchen) |
| ❌ Generic promotion (% off) | 🔽 Menu complexity (30 items → 15 core + 5 seasonal) |

| RAISE | CREATE |
|-------|--------|
| 🔼 Presentation quality (Instagram-worthy) | ✨ "Phở Experience" - customizable broth levels |
| 🔼 Ingredient transparency (organic, local source) | ✨ Subscription model (Phở Pass) |
| 🔼 Digital engagement (app, loyalty) | ✨ Phở DIY Kits (delivery/take-home) |
| 🔼 Delivery quality (special packaging) | ✨ Corporate catering packages |

### 3. New Value Proposition

**Before**: "Fresh, modern phở in nice space"  
**After**: "Personalized phở experience with tech-enabled convenience"

**Key Innovations**:
1. **Phở Customization**: Choose broth richness (light/medium/rich), protein variations, herb selection
2. **Phở Pass**: Subscription 999k/tháng = unlimited phở (1 bowl/day)
3. **Phở DIY Kit**: Pre-portioned ingredients, cook at home (150k/kit, serves 2)
4. **Corporate Phở**: Bulk orders cho văn phòng, delivered hot, branded packaging

## III. PRICING STRATEGY REDESIGN

### Current Pricing Issues

**Problem 1**: Single pricing → không tối ưu revenue
- Phở bò: 65k (flat price mọi size, mọi topping)
- Customer order "no topping" vẫn 65k → không fair
- Customer muốn "extra everything" vẫn 65k → leave money on table

**Problem 2**: Không có bundle/upsell
- Average bill: 68k (chỉ phở)
- No incentive order thêm drinks, appetizers

### New Pricing Architecture

#### 1. Base + Add-ons Model (Modular Pricing)

**Base Phở** (40k):
- Broth (standard richness)
- Noodles (standard portion)
- Herbs

**Add-ons**:
| Item | Price |
|------|-------|
| Beef (rare) | +15k |
| Beef (well-done) | +15k |
| Beef (flank) | +18k |
| Beef (tendon) | +18k |
| Combination (3 meats) | +45k |
| Extra noodles | +10k |
| Organic herbs | +5k |
| Rich broth (+30% bone) | +8k |

**Example Combos**:
- "Classic": Base + rare beef = 55k (cheaper than before!)
- "Deluxe": Base + combination + rich broth = 93k (premium tier)
- "Light": Base only = 40k (budget option)

**Psychology**: 
- Perceived control (customize)
- Fair pricing (pay for what you eat)
- Upsell opportunity (premium options)

#### 2. Bundle Pricing

| Bundle | Content | Price | Savings |
|--------|---------|-------|---------|
| **Quick Lunch** | Phở (base+meat) + trà đá + chả giò | 68k | -10k |
| **Hearty Meal** | Phở deluxe + nem + dessert | 115k | -15k |
| **Couple Combo** | 2 Phở + 2 drinks + appetizer | 125k | -20k |

#### 3. Subscription: "Phở Pass"

**Tier 1 - Daily Phở** (999k/tháng):
- 1 bowl/day (base + 1 meat choice)
- Valid 30 days
- Transferable (dùng cho khách or nhân viên)
- Target: Office workers, students

**Math**:
- 999k ÷ 22 working days = 45k/bowl
- Restaurant COGS: 28k/bowl
- Gross profit: 17k/bowl × 22 = 374k/member

**Benefits**:
- ✅ Predictable revenue (upfront payment)
- ✅ Customer lock-in (frequent visits)
- ✅ Cross-sell (40% will order extras)
- ✅ Cash flow positive

**Target**: Convert 50 subscribers by Month 9 → 50M revenue + loyalty

#### 4. Dynamic Pricing (Off-peak Promotion)

**Problem**: Empty restaurant 3PM-5PM, 8PM-10PM

**Solution**: Off-peak pricing via app
- Book before 2PM for 3-5PM slot: -15%
- Book after 8PM: -10%
- Auto-applied via QR order system

**Expected Impact**: Increase off-peak traffic 30% → improve kitchen utilization

## IV. DIGITAL MARKETING & GROWTH HACKING

### Current Situation (After 6 months)

| Metric | Current | Industry Avg | Gap |
|--------|---------|--------------|-----|
| Instagram followers | 1,200 | 5,000 | -76% |
| Google rating | 4.2 stars | 4.5 stars | -0.3 |
| Repeat customer rate | 18% | 35% | -17pp |
| Delivery % revenue | 15% | 30% | -15pp |
| CAC (Customer Acq Cost) | 280k | 150k | +87% |

**Diagnosis**: Poor digital presence & retention

### Digital Marketing Roadmap

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 7-8)

**1. Website + App**:
- Simple ordering web app (PWA)
- QR code tại table → menu → order → pay
- Loyalty points integration

**2. Social Media Revamp**:
- Instagram:
  - 5 posts/week (food porn, behind-the-scenes, customer stories)
  - Reels 3x/week (cooking process, customer reactions)
  - Stories daily (menu of the day, polls)
- TikTok:
  - Launches TikTok page
  - 1 viral challenge/month (#PhoOiChallenge)
  - Partner với food bloggers (20-100k followers)
- Facebook:
  - Groups engagement (Hanoi Foodies, University groups)
  - Facebook Live: Chef cooking sessions

**Budget Month 7-8**: 20M (10M ads + 10M influencers)

#### Phase 2: Growth Hacking (Month 9-10)

**Tactic 1: Referral Program**
```
Invite friend → Both get 20k credit
Friend orders → You get 10% of their order as credit (forever)
```
**Target**: Each customer refers 2 → viral coefficient 2.0

**Tactic 2: UGC (User Generated Content) Campaign**
```
Post Phở Ơi photo/video với #PhoOiExperience
→ Tag @pho_oi
→ Get 25k credit
→ Best content: Free Phở for 1 month
```
**Target**: 500+ UGC posts → organic reach 100k+

**Tactic 3: Corporate Partnership**
- Approach 20 companies within 2km
- Offer: First order -30%, then 10% permanent discount for employees
- Target: 10 companies × 50 employees = 500 potential regular customers

**Tactic 4: Lunch Subscription Flash Sale**
```
First 100 subscribers: 799k instead of 999k (200k off)
Create urgency (countdown timer)
Launch via Instagram + Facebook ads
```

#### Phase 3: Retention & LTV Optimization (Month 11-12)

**Loyalty Program Structure**:

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Bronze** | 0-5 visits | 5% off, birthday gift |
| **Silver** | 6-20 visits | 10% off, priority seating, early menu access |
| **Gold** | 21-50 visits | 15% off, free delivery, free upgrade, exclusive events |
| **Platinum** | 50+ visits | 20% off, VIP line, coffee/tea free |

**Re-engagement Automation**:
- Không order trong 14 days → SMS: "We miss you! 15% off next visit"
- Không order trong 30 days → Call: Personal check-in + special offer
- Churn (60 days) → Win-back campaign: 50% off or free delivery

## V. CUSTOMER LIFETIME VALUE (CLV) ANALYSIS

### Current CLV (Before Changes)

**Assumptions**:
- Average order value: 68k
- Purchase frequency: 1.2 times/month
- Customer lifespan: 8 months (low due to weak retention)
- Gross margin: 60%

```
CLV = AOV × Frequency × Lifespan × Margin
    = 68k × 1.2 × 8 × 60%
    = 392k per customer
```

**CAC**: 280k  
**LTV:CAC Ratio**: 392k/280k = **1.4** (❌ Below healthy 3.0 threshold)

### Target CLV (After Implementing Strategies)

**Improved Assumptions**:
- Average order value: 85k (bundles, add-ons, upsells)
- Purchase frequency: 2.5 times/month (subscription + loyalty)
- Customer lifespan: 18 months (better retention from loyalty program)
- Gross margin: 62% (better pricing, reduced waste)

```
New CLV = 85k × 2.5 × 18 × 62%
        = 2,363k per customer
```

**New CAC** (with referrals & organic): 180k  
**New LTV:CAC Ratio**: 2,363k/180k = **13.1** (✅ Excellent!)

### CLV Improvement Drivers

| Driver | Impact | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **Increase AOV** (+25%) | +392k → +490k | Modular pricing, bundles |
| **Increase Frequency** (+108%) | 1.2 → 2.5/mo | Subscription, loyalty program |
| **Increase Lifespan** (+125%) | 8 → 18 months | Retention campaigns, tier benefits |
| **Improve Margin** (+2pp) | 60% → 62% | Pricing optimization, waste reduction |
| **Reduce CAC** (-36%) | 280k → 180k | Referrals, organic social |

**Total CLV Increase**: **+503%** (392k → 2,363k)

## X. LESSONS FOR OTHER SME F&B

### 1. Don't Compete, Create New Value Space
Traditional phở đông khách nhưng giá thấp, margin thấp. Modern restaurant margin cao nhưng không thể scale. **Phở Ơi tạo space mới**: quality + tech + subscription.

### 2. Pricing is Strategy, Not Just Math
Flat pricing simple nhưng leave money on table và không fair. **Modular pricing** creates tiers, increases AOV, và cho customer control.

### 3. Subscription = Game Changer for F&B
Recurring revenue, customer lock-in, predictable cash flow. **Critical**: Design để không cannibalize margins (base-only Pass, upsell add-ons).

### 4. Digital is Not Optional
Gen Z + Millennials expect: QR ordering, loyalty app, social presence, convenient payment. **Traditional + Digital** wins.

### 5. Focus on LTV, Not Just Traffic
Dễ chase traffic với promotions, khó build retention. **Loyal customers** worth 10x new customers.
